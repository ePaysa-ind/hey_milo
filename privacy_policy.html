Privacy Policy for Milo App

Last Updated: May 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Milo App ("we," "our," or "us"). Milo App is designed to help seniors manage memories, medications, and caregiver communications. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and storage.

Information We Don't Collect

Milo App functions without collecting, storing, or transmitting your personal data to our servers. Specifically:

  • We do not require users to create accounts or sign up
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • We do not have access to any data stored in the app
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics to track your app usage behavior
  • We do not collect or store voice recordings
  • We do not access your medication information

How Your Data is Stored

All data generated within Milo App (such as voice memories, medication records, and caregiver messages) is stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • In your personal cloud storage if you choose to enable cloud backup

We have no access to this information, nor do we maintain copies of it on our servers.

App Permissions

Milo App may request the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • Microphone: Required to record voice memories
  • Storage: Required to save recordings and other data on your device
  • Notifications: Required for medication reminders

These permissions are used exclusively for the app's functionality and not for data collection purposes.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to use cloud backup features, your data will be stored according to your cloud provider's privacy policy (such as Google Drive or iCloud). We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information on how they handle your data.

Milo App does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Milo App is designed for use by seniors and their caregivers. We do not knowingly collect or solicit data from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
